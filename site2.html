Oswego Historical Society
Walker case

Located in a former Methodist church, the Town of Oswego Historical Society and Museum has many artifacts of Dr. Mary Walker such as photos and letters, and many of her family's items, including her father’s journal, mother’s portrait and sisters’ letters. Other interesting artifacts include her invitation to President Ulysses S. Grant's first inaugural ball, where she sat with Generals Thomas and Sherman, a bible that she purchased from the Lacy mansion in Virginia when she was serving in the hospital there and a medal given posthumously by the National Women’s Hall of Fame in Seneca Falls, NY. Although Mary was never associated with any particular church, she would have visited here, and the minister of this church officiated at her funeral. Open by appointment by calling the Town Historian George DeMass. See the Town of Oswego website for more information.
