Burial Place of Mary Walker

Oswego Historical Society
Walker Family Cemetary
Walker Headstone

Drive through the southernmost entrance on cemetery road - the one with two stone columns on either side. Follow that road all the way to the oldest section in the back of the cemetery until you see a small area surrounded by a white picket fence. A tall obelisk marks the Walker family plot that also contains her mother, father and sister Cynthia who died young. A Historic Marker and a new headstone honor Dr. Mary Walker's burial, though the original stone, a modest flat marker with just the name "Mary" on it rests to the right of the larger headstone. Mary’s new headstone was dedicated in the 1980’s and paid for by her great grandniece Helen Hay Wilson.
